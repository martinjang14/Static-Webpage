  • Rubber ducks were originally made of hard, solid rubber, and were marketed as chew toys.
  • Rubber ducks are so popular, there are hundreds of races held in the USA and internationally.
  • The largest rubber duck collection has over 5,000 ducks, by Charlotte Lee.
  • The largest single rubber duck was over 100 ft long and weighed over 1,000 lbs.
  • Thousands of rubber ducks are sold every week.
  • January 13th is National Rubber Duck Day
  • Many modern versions of rubber ducks are often made of vinyl or plastic.
  • In 2013, a giant rubber duck floated in Victoria Harbor, Hong Kong.
  • The rubber duck was inducted into the Toy Hall of Fame in 2013.
  • The longest rubber duck journey was over 26,000 miles in 2001. It was used to study ocean currents.
